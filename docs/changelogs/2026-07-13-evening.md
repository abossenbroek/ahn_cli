Stakeholder decisions (via AskUserQuestion):
- **Keep NAP** (Netherlands-specific), documented loud and clear. → **NAP-native**, not the

- **Loudest documentation:** prose everywhere + `vertical_datum` in `provenance.json` + a

**`.hf` v3 header (still 120 bytes):** `version`=3; old `pad`@116 removed; `vertical_datum`
(uint32 EPSG code, **5709 = NAP height**) at offset **112**; `header_crc32` moves to **116**,
CRC span grows to `[0,116)` so the datum tag is integrity-protected. Fields `[0,112)` keep
offsets. `region[4]/[5]` become NAP min/max (were ellipsoidal).

**Architectural note:** the region's *height component* becomes profile-dependent (NAP for
heightfield, ellipsoidal for the rest). Region horizontals (lon/lat) are datum-independent and
unchanged. Implementing via a clean per-profile region-height seam (NAP = the tile's own
